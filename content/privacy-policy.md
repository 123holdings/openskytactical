## Privacy Policy

OpenSky Tactical is committed to protecting your information and maintaining operational security.

### Data Collection

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our contact form, including name, organization, email address, and message content.

### Data Use

Submitted information is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry and is never sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes.

### Security

All data transmissions use TLS 1.3 encryption. Form submissions are processed securely and stored in an access-controlled environment.

### Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at ops@opensky.tactical.
